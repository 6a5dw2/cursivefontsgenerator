Choose a readable style

Script Cursive has the most traditional handwritten appearance, while Bold Script makes short words stand out. Italic and Sans Italic are restrained choices for longer sentences. Bold Italic adds weight without many decorative details, and Fraktur offers a dramatic vintage look. Preview the full phrase rather than judging a style from one letter because spacing and capital shapes can change the result. The best cursive fonts stay legible at the size where you will publish them. Try two options, paste both into the destination, and compare them before updating an important public name or profile.
