Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating and using cursive Unicode text.

What is a cursive font generator?

A cursive font generator converts ordinary letters and numbers into similar-looking Unicode characters. The result is real text you can copy and paste, not an image or a downloaded font.

Where can I use the generated text?

You can paste it into Instagram bios, TikTok captions, Discord names, WhatsApp messages, game profiles, documents, and most websites that support Unicode.

Is this cursive text generator free?

Yes. All 38 styles are free to use, with no sign-up, payment, download, or usage limit.

Why do some letters or numbers stay unchanged?

Some Unicode style sets do not contain a special version of every character. When no reliable equivalent exists, the generator keeps the original character so your text remains readable.

Does the generator store what I type?

No. Every conversion happens locally in your browser. The page does not send your text to an API or external service.
